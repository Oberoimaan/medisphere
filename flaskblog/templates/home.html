MediSphere

Disease Diagnosis using Machine Learning diagnoses disease based on the symptoms that the user enters. We diagnose the disease if the patient is not in danger and the user simply wants to know what disease he or she might have. The dataset consisting of at least 100 symptoms, is used to train the model to diagnose various diseases. The web application provide preventive information about common diseases.
